## Version Semantics
- `INSTANCE_DESIGN_VERSION` in `VERSION` tracks the maturity of the product design.
- `PRODUCT_VERSION` in `VERSION` tracks the maturity of the implemented product.
- Changelog entries should make it clear whether a change is primarily design-facing, implementation-facing, or both.
